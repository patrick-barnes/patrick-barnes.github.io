# Explorer Heavy Job Titles

## Overview

If you want an Explorer-dominant career, the trick is knowing the actual job titles companies use. Many of these roles exist, but they’re hidden behind titles that don’t obviously mean “explore new things.”

Below are 10 LinkedIn-searchable job titles that usually contain a lot of exploration.

## 10 Job Titles for Explorer-Type Careers

1. Emerging Technology Architect

Often found in large tech companies or CTO offices.

What you do

- investigate new technologies
- run experiments / proofs of concept
- advise leadership on trends

Typical employers:

- Amazon
- Microsoft
- IBM

Explorer level: high


2. Principal Technologist

This role exists in some organizations as a “technology explorer.”

Activities include:

- evaluating new architectures
- researching emerging tech
- mentoring engineers

Explorer level: high


3. Technology Scout

This role literally exists to find new technologies before competitors do.

Typical activities:

- monitor startups
- attend conferences
- read research papers
- test new tools

Explorer level: very high


4. Venture Capital Technical Partner

Many venture capital firms hire experienced engineers to evaluate startups.

Example firms:

- Andreessen Horowitz
- Sequoia Capital

Explorer level: very high


5. Strategic Foresight Analyst

These roles focus on understanding future trends.

Typical work:

- research emerging technologies
- analyze social change
- build future scenarios

Organizations:

- Institute for the Future
- World Economic Forum

Explorer level: very high


6. Research Engineer

Found in research labs.

Typical activities:

- reading research papers
- prototyping ideas
- experimenting with algorithms

Examples:

- OpenAI
- DeepMind

Explorer level: moderate to high


7. Technology Strategy Lead

These roles evaluate technology trends that could affect the company.

Typical activities:

- tech landscape analysis
- competitor analysis
- architecture strategy

Explorer level: moderate to high


8. Innovation Architect

Some companies have innovation groups that explore new ideas.

Typical work:

- running innovation labs
- prototyping new products
- researching trends

Explorer level: high


9. Technical Fellow

Large organizations sometimes create fellow roles for senior technologists.

These roles allow someone to:

- explore new technologies
- guide company direction
- publish research

Explorer level: moderate to high


10. Developer Advocate

This role involves exploring technology and sharing what you learn.

Typical work:

- experiment with new tools
- build demos
- write tutorials
- speak at conferences

Examples:

- Stripe
- Cloudflare

Explorer level: moderate

## A Trick for Finding These Jobs on LinkedIn

Try searching combinations like:

"emerging technology"
"innovation lab"
"strategic foresight"
"technology scout"
"principal technologist"
"CTO office"

Many of the best Explorer jobs live inside CTO offices or innovation labs.
